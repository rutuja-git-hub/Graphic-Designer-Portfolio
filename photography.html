Photography

It all began in my childhood when I used to roam in my city's zoo with my father's point and shoot digital camera to capture all kinds of animals present. Immersed in the knowledge of different species of animals present, I always wanted have a photograph of each present. This encouraged me more in wildlife photography and I would always keep a camera handy while travelling. Even when I travel today, the fauna of a place always keeps me intrigued.
